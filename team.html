Ganesh Chandramouli

Ganesh is a first class graduate from Bombay University (B.Com) and is a member of The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India.

Ganesh has gained experience in systems studies, management consultancy assignments, internal audit assignments and business advisory services. He has handled various assignments in the areas of Internal Audits and systems improvement. He has gained valuable expertise in restructuring operations, fraud control and development of costing and budgeting models for complex diversified industries.

He has handled a wide range of clients including multinationals and Indian limited companies. Previously he worked with Powerweave Software Services Pvt Ltd (a leading company in b2b solutions) as Manager-Accounts & Finance. He has handled assignments for various software companies in the areas of information risk management and business consultancy and his focus areas of practice are Exchange Control Regulations, STPI & Customs Procedures with respect to 100 % EOU units.

Kunal Mangal

Kunal is a first class graduate from Mumbai University (B.Com) and is a member of The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India. Kunal, a young and a rising professional stood 4th all in India in his CA Final Attempt in May 2013.

Kunal has a demonstrated history of working in the tax practice industry. He is highly skilled in Tax Litigation service, External CFO service, Investment Advisory, Transaction Advisory Services and Project Finance. He has handled work In Industries spanning across Information Technology Enabled Services (ITES), Garment Manufacturing, Real Estate, Large Size Retail Traders and Medical Industry. He has also done Certified Public Accountant from USA, Bachelor of Laws and Bachelor of Commerce from Mumbai University.
