Ganesh and Kunal Tax & Regulatory services

Direct Taxes

The taxation laws are getting more and more complex and the recent times have shown an increasing trend of business entities relying on tax professionals for Tax Planning and advisory related services. It is our endeavor to formulate an ideal tax strategy for our clients which is compliant with the law, legitimately reduces the tax bills and yet is not prone to invite litigation.


Our regulatory compliance services ensure that the clients attain a high level of stringent tax law compliances.

  • Tax Strategy and Compliance
  • Preparation & Filing of Return of Income
  • Representation before Income Tax Officers
  • Certifications under the Income Tax Act
  • Preparation and filing of appeals before the Commissioner & Tribunal (ITAT)
  • Taxation & Legal Opinions
  • Withholding Tax - eTDS compliance

Indirect Taxes

The laws relating to indirect taxation in India are in the state of evolution with constant reforms, alterations and amendments. Further, these indirect taxes are levied at the National level as well as the State level, making their compliance more complicated for the management.

We offer a wide area of services for indirect taxes at the State as well as National Level, helping the management to be updated with the evolving laws and stay focused on the business of the entity.


Our Indirect Tax Practice covers Service Tax & Excise under Central Government Law and Value Added Tax & Profession Tax under State Government Laws.

  • Filing of Returns under respective statutes
  • Preparation & Filing of Return of Income
  • Representation before tax officers
  • Preparation and filing of appeals before the Commissioner & Tribunal
  • Certifications
  • Taxation & Legal Opinions
  • Registration under the GST Act
