G&K

We are a firm of Chartered Accountants offering professional services in the areas of Direct & Indirect Taxation, Audit & Assurance, Regulatory & Compliance and Business Advisory. Established in 2014 in Navi Mumbai, the Firm has 2 partners and a team of over 8 professionals and interns

With a combination of in-house specialized teams we offer a single window for all finance, compliance and accounting related services. It has always been our endeavor to provide with personalized and practical solutions for wide-ranging business and finance issues.
